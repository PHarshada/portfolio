A graduate of Glasgow School of Art, I am a graphic designer and illustrator.

My experiences span branding, editorial, packaging, advertising and design thinking with the work mainly leaning towards social issues. I am also an educator, currently teaching foundation year design students at CEPT University, Ahmedabad.

My personal work is artistic and experiments with ink on paper, trying to depict emotions through a personal abstract style.
